GDPR & CCPA POLICY

Effective Date: September 28, 2025

PRIVACY RIGHTS COMPLIANCE

Be Socially Smashing complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) regarding your personal data rights.

GDPR RIGHTS (EU RESIDENTS)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

●        Access your personal data we process

●        Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data

●        Erase your personal data ("right to be forgotten")

●        Restrict processing of your data

●        Data portability (receive data in structured format)

●        Object to processing based on legitimate interests

●        Withdraw consent for data processing

 

CCPA RIGHTS (CALIFORNIA RESIDENTS)

Under CCPA, California residents have the right to:

●        Know what personal information we collect and how it's used

●        Access your personal information

●        Delete your personal information

●        Opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)

●        Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

CPRA AMENDMENTS (EFFECTIVE 2023)

The California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) expanded CCPA rights. California residents also have the right to:

●        Correct inaccurate personal information

●        Limit the use and disclosure of sensitive personal information

●        Opt-out of the sharing of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising

Note: Be Socially Smashing does not collect sensitive personal information (such as precise geolocation, racial/ethnic origin, health data, biometric data, or similar categories) and does not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

OTHER US STATE PRIVACY RIGHTS

In addition to California residents, residents of Virginia (VCDPA), Colorado (CPA), Connecticut (CTDPA), Utah (UCPA), and other states with comprehensive privacy laws have similar rights, including:

●        Confirm whether we process your personal data

●        Access and obtain a copy of your personal data

●        Correct inaccuracies in your personal data

●        Delete your personal data

●        Opt-out of targeted advertising and sale of personal data

●        Obtain a copy of your personal data in a portable format

Be Socially Smashing honors privacy rights for residents of all US states with applicable privacy legislation.

 

DATA WE COLLECT

For our collaborative consulting services, we may collect:

●        Contact information (name, email, phone)

●        Business information (company name, website URL)

●        Professional background for AI Search Assessment and/or report

●        Communication records from consulting sessions

●        Website usage data and analytics

●        Payment information (processed by third parties)

We do not collect sensitive personal information, including but not limited to: precise geolocation data, racial or ethnic origin, religious beliefs, health information, biometric data, genetic data, sex life or sexual orientation information, or citizenship/immigration status.

 

LAWFUL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

We process data based on:

●        Consent for marketing communications

●        Contract performance for consulting services

●        Legitimate interests for business operations

●        Legal compliance requirements

 

DATA SHARING

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with:

●        Service providers (email, analytics, payment processing)

●        Legal authorities when required by law

●        Business successors in case of acquisition
